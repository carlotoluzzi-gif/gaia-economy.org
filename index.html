The Problem

The world is upside down.
Gaia turns it right side up.

Fifty years of carbon taxes, recycling campaigns, and climate summits have not reversed ecological collapse. Because they are all fighting symptoms. The root cause is a monetary system whose incentives point in the wrong direction. Under Gaia, the price finally tells the truth.
